Privacy Policy｜Switzerland 2026 Guide

Last updated: 2026-05-13

Switzerland 2026 Guide is a travel planning and itinerary application. This policy explains what data the app uses and how it is handled.

Data We Store On Device

The app may store checklist states, form inputs, and preference flags locally on your device using browser localStorage or equivalent WebView storage. This data is used only to keep your itinerary checklist and booking notes available between sessions.

Data We Do Not Collect By Default

The app does not require account registration and does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, health data, or payment card details by default.

External Links

The app contains links to third-party services such as airline, hotel, booking, LINE, and travel pass websites. When you open those links, the third party may collect data according to its own privacy policy.

Payments

If premium digital content is enabled in a Google Play distributed version, purchases must be processed by Google Play Billing. The app itself does not process or store payment card information.

Medical / Wellness Content

Wellness and environment-medicine content is educational and is not medical advice. Any clinic consultation or external service is handled outside this app by the relevant provider.

Retention And Deletion

Locally stored checklist data remains on your device until you clear app storage, uninstall the app, or use any in-app reset feature provided in future versions.
