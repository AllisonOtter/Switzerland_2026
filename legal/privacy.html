Privacy Policy｜Switzerland 2026 Guide

Last updated: 2026-05-13

Switzerland 2026 Guide is a public travel planning website. This policy explains what data the site uses and how it is handled.

Data We Store On Device

The site may store checklist states and preference flags locally in your browser using localStorage. This data is used only to keep simple page preferences available between visits.

Data We Do Not Collect By Default

The site does not require account registration and does not collect precise location, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, health data, or payment card details.

External Links

The site contains links to third-party services such as airline, hotel, booking, and travel pass websites. When you open those links, the third party may collect data according to its own privacy policy.

Travel Content

Travel rhythm, altitude, sleep, hydration, and route notes are general planning information only. Always verify important decisions with official providers and qualified professionals where appropriate.

Retention And Deletion

Locally stored checklist data remains in your browser until you clear browser storage.
